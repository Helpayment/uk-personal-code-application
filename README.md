## What is a Personal Code?

A **Personal Code** is a unique identity reference number issued by Companies House after a person's identity has been verified. It is now a **legal requirement** under the Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (ECCTA 2023).

From **8 April 2024**, all new directors and PSCs must verify their identity when incorporating a company or becoming an officer. From **18 November 2025**, all existing directors, PSCs, and company officers filing a Confirmation Statement (CS01) must include a verified Personal Code.

**Without a Personal Code:**
- New company incorporations will be rejected
- Confirmation Statements (CS01) filed on or after 18 Nov 2025 will be rejected
- Appointments of new directors/PSCs will fail at Companies House

## Who Needs to Verify?

You need a Personal Code if you are:

- **Company director** of a UK limited company (Ltd) or public company (PLC)
- **Person with Significant Control (PSC)** / Beneficial owner of a UK company
- **Company secretary** registered at Companies House
- **LLP member** or designated member
- **Registered overseas entity (ROE)** officer

This applies to both **UK nationals and foreign nationals worldwide**.

## Deadlines — When Do You Need to Verify?

The deadline is **not** a fixed date for everyone. Your deadline depends on when you file your next **Confirmation Statement (CS01)**:

- If your CS01 is due **before 18 November 2025** — you must verify before filing
- If your CS01 is due **after 18 November 2025** — your filing will be rejected if you have not verified

**Your real deadline is your next Confirmation Statement date, not a universal November 2026 deadline.**

→ [Check your CS01 due date on Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/)

## About the ECCTA 2023 Requirement

The **Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (ECCTA 2023)** introduced mandatory identity verification for all registered company officers in the UK. The goal is to reduce corporate fraud and improve transparency of beneficial ownership.

Key dates:
- **8 April 2024** — Identity verification mandatory for new incorporations
- **18 November 2025** — Identity verification mandatory in all CS01 filings
- **Ongoing** — All existing directors and PSCs must verify within their filing cycles
